FAQ's

  • How long does it take for an application to be pre-approved?

      The application process usually takes 1-2 weeks. However, applications accompanied by a location address and site plan may be expedited. The location must be a prime location, approved for a FROOTALITY business by the landlord or Property Management Company.

  • What is the recommended location size?

      There are two franchise models:

      OTC (Over the counter) - Area: 150-200 sft carpet. Desired location: Shopping malls and IT companies

      High Street Model: Area: 600- 800 sft carpet. Desired location: prime location on a high street

  • What are the average costs for owning a FROOTALITY franchise?

      The basic average franchise costs are:

      OTC Model(Over the Counter):
      • Franchise fee: 8,00,000 (Eight Lakhs only)
      • Build cost with equipment: 1000000 lakhs (actual costs depend on site size, location, and condition)
      • Royalty fee 4% gross sales monthly
      • Marketing fee 1% gross sales monthly
      High Street Model:
      • Franchise fee: 20,00,000 (Twenty lakhs only)
      • Build cost with equipment: 1000000-1400000 lakhs (actual costs depend on site size, location, and condition)
      • Royalty fee 4% gross sales monthly
      • Marketing fee 1% gross sales monthly
  • Do you franchise internationally?

      Yes, please fill in the Franchise form to ask about your preferred Master franchisee opportunity and Locations.

  • Do I need experience in the FROOTALITY Industry?

      No. Success in our business requires management and people development skills. FROOTALITY is built by providing a distinctive level of service and focus toward our customers. You must have a genuine liking for people and the sincerity to keep your regular customers and win new ones.

  • What qualifications must I have to be a FROOTALITY Franchisee?
      • Good character
      • Good credit
      • The required amount of capital
      • The desire to serve customers
      • The attitude that your customers are part of your extended family "People" skills
      • A deep desire to succeed by being a part of the FROOTALITY family
  • How long will it take to open a store?

      Once a location is secured and the construction permit has been issued, construction usually takes 3-4 weeks but may vary, depending on location, building requirements and store size.

  • Do you provide Training?

      FROOTALITY is committed to providing intensive training to its Franchisees and Managers. This is done at our Center Of Excellence(COE). Franchisee training, which will take 15 days, will include but not be limited to, aspects of store management. All aspects of product preparation ,serving, merchandising, local advertising ,promotion, public relations, all staffing and human resource issues, franchisee accounting and reporting and use of the computer for administrative functions.

  • Who pays for this Training?

      Training and all course material is provided to you as part of your FROOTALITY Franchise. You will need to budget for travel and accommodation during the two weeks you are attending the training, if applicable.

      How will you help market and promote my store?

      The most important and effective way to promote your store is by "word of mouth". That is what FROOTALITY is all about – treating the customer's right so that they tell all their friends and they come back with their friends. With respect to Advertising, we know the types of advertising that work and the type of media to place it in. We are also skilled at finding ways to help you promote and publicize your location.

  • Do I need experience in the FROOTALITY Industry?

      No. Success in our business requires management and people development skills. FROOTALITY is built by providing a distinctive level of service and focus toward our customers. You must have a genuine liking for people and the sincerity to keep your regular customers and win new ones.

  • Can I transfer or sell my franchise?

      Yes. You own your franchise business and can sell or transfer it. We have the first right of refusal to buy it from you and if we waive that right, we need to approve the purchaser to ensure that they will operate the franchise at least as well as you did.

  • Are there standard operating procedures in place? How do I maintain quality control?

      Consistency in product offering, store design and customer service is essential to the success of all franchisees. All franchisees receive the same training and the same set of Operating Manuals. Our Managers are responsible for working with you to ensure consistency and to help you become more profitable.

  • Is a FROOTALITY franchise a sure thing?

      Nothing in life is guaranteed. That applies to your health, your marriage, your job or your business. It is your personal effort and commitment, together with our momentum, support and expertise that will maximize your chances of being successful and having fun.

  • Where do I get all my supplies, products and equipment?

      Everything is purchased from designated vendors, at the best pricing available for the volumes that the entire network is doing with that vendors.

  • How much money can I make as a FROOTALITY franchisee?

      We can't tell you how much money you will make. It depends on a lot of things. We can however tell you precisely how you can make money as a FROOTALITY franchisee.
